ABOUT

I was once told that you I have two choices when picking a location to live. I could either choose the mountains or the beach. I grew up in Cape Cod so the beach was always essentially my back yard. Fortunately for me, New England has some pretty great mountains that are not too far of a car ride away. So I split my time at the beach during the summers and took to the mountains during the winter.

After I graduated college, I decided to move out to Colorado, where I lived for about 4 years before moving to Western Massachusetts. I currently work as a graphic designer for Parlor Skis and as a Production Artist/Screen Printer for Soundscape Merchandise.

Outside of work I play rugby, surf, snowboard, and play pool.
